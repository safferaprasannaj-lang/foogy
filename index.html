FOODORA

Foodora is an innovative food delivery application specially designed to make food ordering simple, accessible, and independent for visually impaired users. Powered by an intelligent AI Voice Assistant, Foodora allows users to browse menus, discover restaurants, and place orders using voice commands instead of relying on visual navigation. The app provides a seamless and user-friendly experience, ensuring that everyone can enjoy convenient food delivery with confidence and ease.

Foodora also offers budget-based food recommendations, helping users find meals that match their spending preferences. By analyzing user requirements, location, and budget, the AI assistant suggests suitable restaurants and dishes while providing voice-guided support throughout the ordering process. With accessibility, affordability, and convenience at its core, Foodora aims to create an inclusive food delivery platform where technology empowers visually impaired individuals to order food independently and effortlessly
